Diploma in Social Work at City College

City College proudly offers a Diploma in Social Work program, designed to prepare compassionate and skilled professionals dedicated to improving the well-being of individuals, families, and communities. This comprehensive program provides a balance of theoretical knowledge and practical fieldwork to equip students with the tools needed to make a meaningful impact. The program spans three years, beginning at Level 4, advancing to Level 5, and culminating at Level 6, with each level comprising two semesters.

Program Structure

  • Academic Calendar: The academic year starts in October and concludes in August, with structured end-of-semester evaluations to ensure academic and professional readiness.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: The curriculum covers diverse topics such as psychology, sociology, social policy, counseling, human rights, and community development, fostering a well-rounded education.

Why Choose Our Diploma in Social Work Program?

1. Experienced Faculty

Our lecturers are seasoned professionals with extensive expertise in social work practice and education. Their dedication ensures students gain both the knowledge and empathy required to excel in this vital field.

2. Practical Field Training

Students engage in supervised fieldwork placements within various community organizations, giving them real-world experience and exposure to diverse social issues.

3. Focus on Community and Advocacy

The program emphasizes advocacy and empowerment, equipping students with the skills to address societal challenges and promote social justice effectively.

4. Career Opportunities

Graduates are well-prepared to pursue careers in areas such as child welfare, community development, healthcare, counseling, and social policy implementation. The program also provides a solid foundation for further studies.

Additional Benefits

  • Modern Learning Resources: City College provides access to well-equipped libraries, research facilities, and digital tools that support academic success.
  • Engaged Learning Environment: From enrollment to graduation, students are actively involved in academic, professional, and community activities, fostering personal and professional growth.
  • Accessible Education: Our commitment to affordability ensures that quality education is within reach for all aspiring social workers.

Enroll Today

By enrolling in the Diploma in Social Work program at City College, students take a significant step toward a fulfilling career in making a difference in society.
